Interesting Facts

The Jacksonville Zoo and Gardens first opened on May 12th, 1914. It spans a total of 115 acres, and is home to more than 2000 animals and 1000 plants. There are typically more than one million visits each year to see the different animals and plants, as well as the marine life, which includes several different types of fish and stingrays.
